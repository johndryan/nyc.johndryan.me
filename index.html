A Summer with UNICEF's Innovation Team

During the summer of 2012, I was selected for the UNICEF/DesignMatter's Fellowship. As a student of ACCD's Media Design Program, I worked as a designer with UNICEF's Innovation Team—who leverage design thinking and technology to solve problems and overcome barriers encountered in UNICEF's global work. This mini-site is an archive of ideas, experiences and insights from both working with this team and living in New York City.
