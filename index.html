A Summer with UNICEF's Innovation Team

During the summer of 2012, I worked as the DesignMatters and Media Design fellow with UNICEF's Innovation Team. The team leverage design thinking and technology to solve problems and overcome barriers encountered in UNICEF's global work.

This mini-site is an archive of ideas, experiences and insights that I gathered during my time with the team and in New York.
